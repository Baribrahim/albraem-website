About Albraem Arabic School

Welcome

Dear Parents and Carers

The Albaraem Arabic School would like to take this opportunity to announce the opening of the Arabic language and Islamic studies classes for the academic year 2024-2025. These classes will run every Sunday 10:00 – 13:30 at Toynbee school; Bodycoats Rd, Chandler's Ford, Eastleigh SO53 2PL. The classes will be run by high experienced professional teachers. Ages from +5 - 16 years are accepted in the school.

Academic Structure

Our Academic Year

The academic year is composed of three terms

01

Term 1

Starts from September to December

*No classes during school half terms and Christmas holiday
02

Term 2

Starts from January to end of March

*No classes during school half terms and Easter break
03

Term 3

Starts immediately when students return after Easter break till end of academic year in July

*We always start on the Sunday after return to school after every holiday
